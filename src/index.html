Bored Worker App

Everyone feels bored from time to time. Imagine there's not much to do at work today.

You have already managed to check out 65 (Productive) Things to Do When You're Bored at Work.

Or perhaps the task you have to do is just too boring and you need a break to take your mind off it.

Our app will help you forget about this unpleasant feeling of boredom at work (well, at least for a while).

Holiday Checker

Maybe you need to have some time off?

Here you can check if the given day is a holiday (unfortunately, not every holiday means free time).

All you have to do is select a date and if it's a holiday the program will return its date and name.

If the date entered is not a holiday, no result will appear.
