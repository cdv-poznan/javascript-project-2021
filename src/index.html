CLASSIFICATION OF COLORING SUBSTANCES

OK, let's figure out what the main means for coloring and discoloring hair are:

TINT PRODUCTS.They are used for short-term giving the hair a new shade. They create a color coating on the surface of the hair, quickly wash off, and have the most gentle effect. They are most noticeable on blond hair â€“ best of all those that belong to the red-orange range, slightly worse are all shades of blue-eggplant. But on dark hair, the effect of applying tint products is almost invisible.

SOFT HAIR DYES.Soft hair dyes contain a gentle oxidizer in a very small concentration. They are more resistant than tint products. They are washed off after 10-20 times: the process is gradual, without the effect of overgrown roots. This dyes will add shine and saturation to the natural color or enliven it with new shades. It is also perfect for toning hair after discoloration.

LONG-LASTING HAIR DYES.These are universal tools that can give any desired effect. But they contain strong oxidizing agents that negatively affect the health of the hair. They are used to obtain a uniform and persistent color when coloring gray hair and to lighten the hair by !ONE or TWO tones. In all other cases, it is better to use more gentle coloring agents. IMPORTANT: HAIR DYE HAIR DYE will not discolor or in simple words - if your hair was colored in a darker color, and you want to make it lighter, then you need to go through the procedure of decolorization.

HAIR BLEACHERS.Hair bleachers are used to bleach (by !THREE to FIVE tones or more) dark hair before using the lightest shades of hair dye. They have a destructive effect on the natural structure of the hair. After their application, it is necessary to use special soft shampoos and balms, as well as means for intensive hair treatment. If you have an undesirable shade of hair, you should perform a hair toning procedure.
